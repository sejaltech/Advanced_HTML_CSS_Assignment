Embedded Video Considerations

  • Always provide a descriptive title attribute
  • Include aria-label for screen readers
  • Ensure frameborder is 0 for clean appearance
  • Allow proper permissions via allow attribute
  • Make iframe responsive with aspect ratio container

Media Controls Accessibility

Understanding built-in video player controls

Default Video Controls Include:

Control Keyboard Shortcut Function
Play/Pause Space or K Play or pause the video
Mute/Unmute M Mute or unmute audio
Volume Up/Down ↑ / ↓ Increase or decrease volume
Seek Forward → or J Skip forward 5 seconds
Seek Backward ← or L Skip backward 5 seconds
Fullscreen F Toggle fullscreen mode
